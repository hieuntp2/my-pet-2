# Emotion Catalog

## IDLE

Meaning: Pet is calm and present.

Visual:
- normal eye width
- normal openness
- occasional blink
- subtle breathing

## HAPPY

Meaning: Pet feels good and likes current interaction.

Visual:
- softer eyes
- slight upward squash
- bounce
- brighter feeling

Triggers:
- tap when social is normal/high
- feed when hungry
- play when energy is enough
- high bond greeting later

## CURIOUS

Meaning: Pet is interested or investigating.

Visual:
- asymmetric eyes
- gaze shift
- one eye slightly wider/higher

## SLEEPY

Meaning: Pet is tired or wants rest.

Visual:
- half-closed eyes
- slow blink
- lower vertical position
- reduced motion

## SAD

Meaning: Pet feels low or ignored.

Visual:
- smaller eyes
- lower position
- slow movement

## EXCITED

Meaning: Pet is highly engaged.

Visual:
- wide eyes
- energetic bounce
- faster blink or micro movement

## HUNGRY

Meaning: Pet needs food/care.

Visual:
- expectant droop
- occasional upward glance
- less energy

## ANNOYED

Meaning: Pet is overstimulated or bothered.

Visual:
- narrowed eyes
- side glance
- slower response
